Learning Plan

Foundations

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long term goal is to get a job as a web developer or elsewhere in the industry. I hope that during my time at Dev Academy, I will form a greater idea of what I want to do specifically as a career.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

One of my strengths is problem solving. I am able to look at problems in different ways to form solutions. Being a problem solver was something that came up a lot when I was researching how to think like a programmer, so I think this will be helpful during the course.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to see myself get better at time management over the course of the next few months. I think that being better at managing my time would be really helpful in all areas of my life. Being able to plan my time better would take a lot of stress off me in work life, but also my personal life as well.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

In order to manage my workload, I will be dividing my work for the week up on a Monday, giving myself a clear overview of what work I have to get done each day. When I am working with others, I will create clear lines of communication at the beginning of the project, so that everyone is clear what they have to do. When things are building up and I am feeling stressed, I will reassess my plan for the week. In doing this I will have a greater idea of when I should be working on different things and it should take some of the pressure off me.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

When I am struggling I will use all available sources of help. To begin with I will make sure I have gone over all of the learning materials to make sure I haven’t missed anything. If I am still unsure, I will reach out to staff and other students in the hope that they will be able to show me the way. I am going to make sure that I don’t stay stuck on something without reaching out for too long so that my workflow is not disrupted.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

I expect helpful guidance from the facilitator team throughout the course. I expect this to come in the form of showing me where I am going wrong and what direction to go in the future without explicitly giving me the solutions.
